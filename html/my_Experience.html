Work Experience

To complete my main course i needed to complete 60 hours of work experience.

Cambridgeshire Community Reuse & Recycling Network.

Before i volunteered I had to Go to their main store in Wisbech for an induction where i was taught their health and safety and their policies.

During my time as a volunteer at Cambridgeshire Community Reuse & Recycling Network I had to work with others to move, label, mix and recycle paint.

The first day I worked I had to move 5Litre bucket of paint for the first half of the day. After, I had to "Condense" paint which was a process were I sorted paints by their colour

Once they were sorted I chose an empty 9Litre bucket of a certain colour then pour recycled 5Litre buckets of the same colour into the 9 litre until it was full.

This is where i logged my hours.
